# Governance Model – Relational Calculus Foundation

## Overview

The Relational Calculus Foundation is an open-source project with a mission to
democratize computational efficiency through Relational Calculus. This document
describes the governance structure that guides the project, ensuring stable
growth and clear decision-making.

## Roles and Responsibilities

### Lead Maintainer (BDFL – Benevolent Dictator for Life)

- **Massimiliano Concas** is the founder and Lead Maintainer of the project.
- He holds final authority on design decisions, acceptance of significant
  contributions, and the overall direction of the project.
- He serves as the public face of the foundation.

### Project Maintainers

- Project Maintainers are trusted contributors with write access to the
  repository.
- They are responsible for reviewing and merging Pull Requests, managing
  issues, and enforcing the Code of Conduct.
- **Appointment:** The Lead Maintainer may appoint new Project Maintainers
  based on merit, expertise, and demonstrated commitment. New Maintainers are
  generally chosen from active contributors who have earned the community's
  trust.
- **Removal:** Maintainer status can be revoked by the Lead Maintainer in case
  of prolonged inactivity, violation of the Code of Conduct, or other serious
  circumstances. A Maintainer may also resign at any time.

### Contributors

- Anyone can contribute to the project.
- Regular, high-quality contributions may lead to an invitation to become a
  Project Maintainer.

## Decision-Making Process

### Day-to-Day Decisions

- Routine tasks (reviewing small PRs, managing issues, documentation
  maintenance) are handled by Project Maintainers.
- For non-trivial PRs, approval from at least one other Maintainer (or the
  Lead Maintainer) is required before merging.

### Strategic Decisions

- Decisions that impact the framework's direction, roadmap, partnerships, or
  the foundation's finances (e.g., allocation of fiscal funds) are made by the
  Lead Maintainer in consultation with Project Maintainers.
- The Lead Maintainer seeks consensus among Maintainers; in case of unresolved
  disagreement, the Lead Maintainer has the final say.

### Appointment of New Maintainers

1. A Contributor who has shown exceptional commitment (several quality
   contributions, constructive participation in discussions, helping other
   users) may be proposed as a Maintainer by an existing Maintainer.
2. The proposal is discussed privately among the Maintainers.
3. If the Lead Maintainer approves, the Contributor is invited to the role.

## Standards of Conduct

All participants must adhere to the [Code of Conduct](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
Violations will be handled according to the guidelines contained therein.

## Changes to This Document

This governance document may be amended by the Lead Maintainer with the
consent of a majority of Project Maintainers. Changes will be communicated
to the community via the repository and official channels.

## Future Evolution

As the community grows, this model may evolve to include more layers of
governance (e.g., a Steering Committee, a Technical Board), always with the
goal of maintaining alignment with the foundation's mission.
